Walking Tips in nature

Walking in nature with your German Shepherd is a great way to keep your dog active, focused, and happy. Natural paths, forests, and open fields give your dog new smells, sounds, and places to explore.

Before going for a longer walk, check the route and make sure it is safe for both you and your dog. Bring enough fresh water, use a comfortable leash, and avoid walking during very hot weather.

It is also important to respect other people, animals, and wildlife around you. Keep your dog under control, clean up after them, and enjoy the time spent together outside.
