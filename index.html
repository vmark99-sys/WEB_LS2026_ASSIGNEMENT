Walking Tips in nature

Spending time in nature with your German Shepherd is a great way to keep your dog active, healthy, and happy. Long walks help build a stronger bond, improve focus, and give your dog a chance to explore new smells and sounds. Always choose safe walking paths, bring enough fresh water, and keep your dog under control around wildlife, cyclists, and other visitors.

Defense training

Defense training helps your German Shepherd build confidence, discipline, and better control in challenging situations. It is important to train step by step, using clear commands, patience, and positive guidance. A well-trained dog should stay focused, listen to the handler, and react only when needed. Always practice in a safe environment and make sure the training is handled responsibly.
