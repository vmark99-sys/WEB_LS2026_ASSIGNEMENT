Defense Training

Defense training helps your German Shepherd build confidence, discipline, and better control in stressful situations. With regular practice, your dog learns to focus on the handler and react only to clear commands.

Training should always start with basic obedience, trust, and calm behavior. A good defense dog must understand when to stay calm, when to listen, and when to stop immediately.

It is important to practice in a safe place and use responsible training methods. Defense training should never make the dog aggressive, but confident, controlled, and reliable.
